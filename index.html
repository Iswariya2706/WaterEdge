The Urban Water Crisis

Cities are running out of groundwater as unchecked concretization and non-scientific planning disrupt natural infiltration and worsen flood risks.

Impervious Plot Layouts

Excessive concrete blocks natural percolation, reducing groundwater recharge and increasing flood risk.

Generic RWH Systems

Rainwater harvesting is often treated as a compliance checkbox, is not site-specific, leading to low efficiency and poor long-term performance.

No Infiltration Mapping Tools

Homeowners and builders have no system that analyses a plot’s natural infiltration potential and guides how much area must remain pervious for sustainable groundwater recharge.

Increasing Urban Flooding

Absence of recharge pits, permeable zones, and green buffers turns rainfall into fast surface runoff.

Reactive Flood Models

Current systems only provide warnings but offer no preventive solutions or actionable insights.

Poor Groundwater Management

Focus on extraction without strategic recharge planning leads to unsustainable water resource depletion.

Intelligent Water Resilience Solution

An AI-powered platform that transforms any plot into a water-positive, climate-resilient site for both groundwater recharge and flood prevention

Soil–Concrete Ratio Optimizer (SCRO)

AI module that calculates the ideal pervious–impervious balance for a specific plot and recommends optimal placement and sizing of recharge pits, rain gardens, permeable pavements, and infiltration zones.

AI Layout & Floor Plan Generator

Creates a full 2-D site and building layout by integrating user inputs with SCRO outputs, ensuring maximum infiltration without compromising structure, usability, or land value.

Dual-Mode Access (Self-Service + Premium Survey)

Users can either generate AI-powered recharge and layout plans instantly through self-service mode or choose a premium on-site survey for enhanced accuracy and tailored recommendations.

Automated Compliance

Generates professional, submission-ready reports and the final layout for municipal approvals, ESG and compilance documentation.

Advanced Technology Architecture

A unified AI-driven system that designs water-positive, flood-resilient plots using geospatial intelligence and automated planning.

Geospatial Intelligence Layer

Processes soil, rainfall, slope, and groundwater patterns to deliver truly site-specific recharge insights.

SCRO Optimization Engine

Calculates the ideal soil–concrete balance and recommends the exact recharge structures needed for the plot.

AI Layout Generator

Converts optimization outputs into complete 2-D layouts that maximize infiltration without reducing land value.

Automated Compliance Reports
