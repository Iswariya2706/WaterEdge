The Urban Water Crisis

Cities face unprecedented water challenges that traditional solutions cannot address

Impervious Surfaces

High concrete coverage disrupts natural water cycles, preventing groundwater recharge and increasing flood risk.

Severe Droughts

2019 Day Zero crisis in Chennai highlighted dangerous over-reliance on groundwater extraction without recharge.

Catastrophic Floods

2015 and 2023 floods revealed inadequate drainage systems and poor flood management infrastructure.

Underutilized Rainwater

Manual rainwater harvesting systems lack enforcement and optimization, missing massive recharge opportunities.

Reactive Flood Models

Current systems only provide warnings but offer no preventive solutions or actionable insights.

Poor Groundwater Management

Focus on extraction without strategic recharge planning leads to unsustainable water resource depletion.
